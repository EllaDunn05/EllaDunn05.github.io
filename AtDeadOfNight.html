At Dead Of Night

Released on the 19th November 2020 At Dead Of Night, a game created by Baggy Cat ltd.

Gameplay

At Dead of Night is a captivating live action, psychological horror game that immerses players in a spine-chilling and suspenseful experience. The gameplay revolves around exploring an eerie and haunted hotel, with each room holding its own dark secrets and supernatural occurrences. As the main protagonist named Maya, players must uncover the mysteries surrounding the hotel and its ghostly inhabitants. Interacting with non-playable characters and gathering clues through intense conversation-based interactions forms a crucial part of the gameplay. Additionally, players must strategically utilize stealth and puzzle-solving skills to evade malevolent spirits and Jimmy Haul who stalk the corridors. The game's unique feature is the innovative use of live-action footage, blending it seamlessly with interactive gameplay elements to enhance the immersion and create a truly hair-raising atmosphere.

Development And Community

One intriguing theory commonly surrounding "At Dead of Night" revolves around the concept of parallel dimensions. According to this subreddit theory, the haunted hotel in the game serves as a portal or intersection point between different dimensions, each representing a unique timeline or reality. As players delve deeper into the mysteries of the hotel, they begin to uncover hints and clues that suggest the presence of alternate versions of characters and events. The shifting nature of the hotel's layout, combined with the eerie occurrences and ghostly apparitions, further support this idea. It is speculated that the protagonist's actions and choices throughout the game may influence which dimension they ultimately inhabit or even alter the course of events in different timelines.

Bugs And Glitches

Some players have reported encountering occasional glitches, such as characters getting stuck in the environment or experiencing unnatural movement patterns. These issues can disrupt the immersion and detract from the overall experience. Additionally, there have been reports of inconsistent AI behavior, where non-playable characters may act in unpredictable ways or fail to respond appropriately to player interactions. Some players have also noted instances of audio and visual synchronization problems, where dialogue lines may not match the character's lip movements or sound effects may be out of sync. While these bugs can be frustrating, it is important to note that the developers have been actively working to address and fix these issues through patches and updates, aiming to provide a smoother and more polished gameplay experience for players to enjoy.
